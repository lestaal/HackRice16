Rules

Gameplay
  • Your goal is to make a tunnel at least 2 blocks wide to the bottom of the screen in order to escape and level up
  • A row of random blocks will generate at the bottom of the screen every once in a while, pushing all blocks towards the top, so move fast!
  • All pieces are the same as classic Tetris except for the black 1x1 bomb piece. This piece destroys every block adjacent to it.
Controls
  • Move falling pieces with the left and right arrow keys
  • Rotate pieces with the up arrow key
  • Hold the down arrow key to accelerate the piece
